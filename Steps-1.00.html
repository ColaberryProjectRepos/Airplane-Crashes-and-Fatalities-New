Messaging Problem Statement 1

Plane crashes are the most disastrous of all transport accidents, largely because they often involve a catastrophic loss of life. One plane crash can kill as many as four or five hundred times more people than a car accident, and when a plane goes down, it can shake an entire nation to its core. There have been many airplane disasters in history, some of which have resulted in a significant loss of life. Some of the deadliest airplane crashes in history include:
The Tenerife airport disaster on March 27, 1977, in which two Boeing 747s collided on a runway in the Canary Islands, Spain, resulting in 583 deaths.
